About Who Are We ?

Herbal lagoons is an enterprise providing premium quality and shelf-stable fruits, vegetables, and herbs to its customers. Dealing with research, production, dehydration, and marking of products. We are committed to providing good taste, healthy and nutrition-loaded food. We are focused to make unremitting efforts to mitigate the problem of perishability and established strong technical and scientific approaches for value-added horticultural products.
Our main products are herbs including oregano, thyme, parsley, rosemary, basil, chamomile, vegetables; Onion, Garlic, ginger, Biter gourd red/green chili, carrot, Tomato, and fruits; Apple, Banana, Mango, Peach, Apricot, Plump, Pomegranate, Oranges, Strawberry (available in the form of flakes, chunks, and powder). These foodstuffs are purely natural, with no additive or synthetic chemicals used for food processing.
Herbal lagoons pounding impact in a dichotomous way, i.e. Short term and Long term.

Short Term Impact

“All-natural” we do not compromise on health, all products are preserved through natural means. Herbal lagoons mainly use solar radiation and air dehydration. Slow air-drying allows the moisture to evaporate leaving all the nutrients intact. These herbs and dehydrated products are full of antioxidants and rich in minerals, vitamins, and other essential diet components. Furthermore, “zero moisture” will ensure “zero diseases” as there will be no microbial activity. Our deft dehydration, proper packaging, and storage increase the shelf life by 2-3 years. These disease-free ingredients will boost the overall health of society. “Smart Chief” these ready-to-use products are smart in their use. No tears or odder issues while dicing onions or garlic etc. Weather its in a home kitchen or as an camping essential, our dried veggies are an amazing addition to every meal. These flakes are easy to carry and cook and will boost your spirit of cooking. You can now make an omelet, soup, casseroles, and many more recipes, in no time because your ingredients are ready to add.

Long Term Impact

Herbal Lagoons is a company having a clear vision and great room for growth and innovation. Following the strategy of Green Aura (GA), we are approaching the baseline farmers. Through contract farming, introducing new horticultural crops in cropping patterns of Baluchistan which ultimately influence the national economy. Import costs will be mitigated due to the self-sustainable production of the herbal plants on various growing sites in Baluchistan. Herbal Lagoons meet the standards and exceed customers’ expectations. We are not only selling products but health in a very nominal economical way and restoring harmony in society through our business venture.
